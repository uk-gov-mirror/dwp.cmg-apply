Benefits of Child Maintenance service

{% if data['safeguarding'] == "Yes" %}

Concerns about your safety

The Child Maintenance Service could be a form of mediation if you have safety concerns. We communicate with you and the other parent separately, and you can manage your case in the My Child Maintenance Case service. We also offer resources in situations of domestic abuse.

{% endif %} {% if data['communication-children'] == "No" %}

Communication with the other parent

By using the Child Maintenance Service, you would not need to talk regularly to the other parent about the case. You could update your details and information that affects payments on the My Child Maintenance Case service. However, you may still need to talk to them about day-to-day care and scheduling visits.

{% endif %} {% if data['communication-finances'] == "No" %}

Communication with the other parent

The Child Maintenance Service uses the Collect and Pay method to transfer money from one parent to the other. We would work with both of you individually to set up a regular payment schedule.

{% endif %} {% if data['bank-details'] == "No" %}

Finances

Because we use the Collect and Pay method to transfer money from one parent to the other, we do not need to tell the other parent about your bank details. This may be a more private option for you.

Care arrangement
